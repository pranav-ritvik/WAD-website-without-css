Benelli Motorcycle


Benelli offers a range of motorcycles that combine Italian craftsmanship with modern technology. Here are some of the standout models:

  • Imperiale 400: A retro-styled cruiser with a 374cc engine, offering 21 bhp and 29 Nm torque. Perfect for those who appreciate classic designs.
  • TRK 502 & 502X: Built for adventurers, these bikes feature a 500cc twin-cylinder engine and options for touring and off-road enthusiasts.
  • 502C: A premium cruiser with a bold design and a powerful 500cc engine, delivering comfort and performance.
  • Leoncino 500: A modern scrambler blending classic aesthetics with versatility, suited for city rides and light off-road adventures.

Benelli is also set to launch exciting new models, including the Leoncino 800 and TNT series, expanding their portfolio in the coming years.
